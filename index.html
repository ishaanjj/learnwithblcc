Who we are

Supporting cultural adaptation through language instruction.

We are Paninna and Jorge Samadder, siblings, who are passionate about sharing our beautiful language and rich culture with people from all walks of life. Whether you're visiting Bangladesh for work, study, or any other reason, we can help you quickly gain the language skills you need to feel at home in your new surroundings.

Since 1998, we have had the pleasure of teaching students from various countries and organizations, including the Korea International Cooperation Agency (Koica), the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA), Peace Corps, Australian Youth Ambassadors for Development (Ayad), Voluntary Service Overseas (VSO) and many others. In addition to teaching adults, we also offer language instruction to children of Bangladeshi expats who want to keep their cultural traditions alive.

Discover Bangladesh


Experience firsthand the culture of Bangladesh



Visit sites of interest with us

  • Explore Dhaka and its history through interesting sites and historical monuments.
  • Discover the breathtaking modernist architecture of the Parliament building
  • Delve into the history of Bangladesh and immerse yourself in the rich and complex narrative of this nation
  • Explore the renowned Hindu town of Dhamrai and its ancient metal casting
  • Spend the day in Sonargaon, the ancient capital of Bengal


Experience Bangladeshi life

  • Explore traditional food and clothes markets where you can try your luck at bargaining for a better price
  • Discover the beautiful and varied crafts shops of Dhaka
  • Shop for traditional Bangladeshi clothing and learn how to wear a sari
  • Visit the markets for seasonal vegetables and learn how to cook authentic Bangladeshi dishes in your own home
  • Visit carefully selected food outlets to get a taste of typical Bangladeshi cuisine

Our pricing & approach

At BLCC, we pride ourselves on offering competitive courses and experiences tailored to your specific needs. For classes, the cost will depend on whether you prefer in-person or virtual instruction and if you are interested in an individual or group setting. For experiences, we charge for our time as well as any additional expenses such as transportation and entry fees to monuments, food, etc.
